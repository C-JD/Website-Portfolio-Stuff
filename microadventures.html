🌅 Dawn Swim at Llangorse Lake

15 March 2025 Wild Swimming 🌫️ Foggy

Location: Llangorse Lake, Brecon Beacons

The mist hung low over the water like a secret being whispered. At 6 AM, I was the only soul brave (or mad) enough to slip into the lake. The cold was shocking but invigorating - like being baptized by the mountains themselves. A heron watched me from the reeds, unimpressed by my splashing. By the time I emerged, the sun had begun to burn through the fog, revealing the Brecon Beacons in all their glory.

⭐ Stargazing at Pen y Fan

8 March 2025 Stargazing ☀️ Clear

Location: Pen y Fan Summit

Climbed up in the dark with just a headtorch and thermos of tea. The effort was worth it - the Milky Way stretched across the sky like spilled flour on a black tablecloth. Saw three shooting stars and made three wishes (one for more adventures like this). The silence up there is profound - you can hear your own heartbeat and the distant bleating of sheep in the valleys below.

🥪 Hilltop Picnic at Castell Coch

2 March 2025 Hilltop Picnic ⛅ Partly Cloudy

Location: Castell Coch, Cardiff

Packed bara brith and Welsh cakes for a fairy-tale picnic beside the red castle. The view over the Taff Valley was spectacular, and I felt like I was dining in a medieval romance novel. A red kite circled overhead, probably hoping for crumbs. Sometimes the best adventures are the ones that make you feel like you're in a story.
